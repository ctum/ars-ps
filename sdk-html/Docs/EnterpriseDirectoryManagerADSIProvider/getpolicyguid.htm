Getting GUID of Policy Entry

This topic explains how to get the GUID of a Policy Entry (Policy) using the Active Roles snap-in.

To get the policy GUID

  1. In the console tree, expand Active Roles/Configuration.

  2. Expand the Policies node, and then expand Administration.

  3. Under Administration, click the folder that contains the Policy Object that includes the policy in question.

  4. In the details pane, right-click that Policy Object, and then on the shortcut menu, click Properties.

  5. On the Policies tab, select the policy in question, and click View/Edit. The Properties dialog box for the selected policy appears.

  6. The GUID of the policy is displayed on the General tab, next to Policy ID.
