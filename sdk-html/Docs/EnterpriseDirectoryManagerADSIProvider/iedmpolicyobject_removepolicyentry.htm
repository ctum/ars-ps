IEDMPolicyObject::RemovePolicyEntryByID

The RemovePolicyEntryByID method removes  the specified Policy from Policy Object. The Policy to be removed is identified with its GUID. 

Sub  RemovePolicyEntryByID(ByVal POE_GUID)

Arguments

POE_GUID

[in] Required. The GUID of the Policy Object Entry you want to remove. To get the GUID, use the GUID property of the IEDMPolicyEntry interface.
